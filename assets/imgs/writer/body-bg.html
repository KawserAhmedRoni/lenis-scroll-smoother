FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick and accessible answers to common inquiries about our services and products. Find the information you need without the hassle of contacting customer support.

How can I purchase a template?

You can browse our collection of templates on our website. Once you find a template you like, you can purchase it directly from ThemeForest through the provided link. After the purchase, you will receive a download link via email.

Can I customize the templates?

Yes, all our templates are fully easily customizable. This means you can modify the design, layout, colors, and content to match your brand’s identity. If you need assistance with customization, we’re always here to help you.

Do you offer support after purchase?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive support for all our templates. If you encounter any issues or have questions, you can reach out to our support team via email or live chat. We also offer detailed documentation and tutorials to help you get started.

Are your templates responsive and mobile-friendly?

Yes, all our templates are designed to be fully responsive and mobile-friendly. They will look great and function seamlessly on any device, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Can I use the templates for multiple projects?

Our standard license allows you to use the template for a single project. If you need to use the template for multiple projects, you can purchase a multi-use license. Please contact our support team for more information on licensing options.

How often do you update your templates?

We regularly update our templates to ensure compatibility with the latest web standards and technologies. Updates are free for all customers who have purchased the template.

Do you offer custom template development?

Yes, we offer custom template development services. If you have specific requirements or need a unique design, our team can create a custom template tailored to your needs. Please contact us for more details.

Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied with the template?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee on all our templates. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund as per ThemeForest policy.
